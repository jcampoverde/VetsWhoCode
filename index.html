About us

Veterans often possess a set of skills that make them uniquely well-suited for success. Recognizing the great potential of our service members, Vets Who Code strives to expand their existing skill set where needed with hands-on, technical, web development training.

Vets Who Code is a social enterprise focusing on teaching Veterans (Army, Navy, Air Force, Marine Corp or US Coast Guard) the most relevant technology skills needed for the current job market in topics including: Web and mobile development, data science, software engineering, digital marketing and more. We are based in Chicago, IL. with trainings held at two BLUE1647 Technology Innovation Center campuses.

For Vets Who Code and BLUE1647, increasing the affordability and accessibility of its programs is a core mission, providing ways to make programming available to more people, especially those who are underrepresented in the industry.

Veterans are some of the most passionate and capable members of our American society. Veterans are encouraged to take advantage of this unique opportunity to gain industry specific technical training in the tech industry, and add their unique perspective to the community.
